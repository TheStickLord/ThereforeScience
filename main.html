Welcome to Therefore Science!

We at Therefore Science strive to better the future of humanity.

Therefore Science initially started as a simple mining company, then under the name Patty's Mining Co. Despite our humble roots, Therefore Science has been innovating since the beginning. The first patent developed by Patty's Mining Co. was a pizza holding device. This device was the groundwork for our Science division, which was founded due to the success of Patty's Pizza Holder.

Since then, we’ve hit the ground running, developing new technology, such as our advanced mining equipment, our FLOOD network, and FLOOD communications devices.
